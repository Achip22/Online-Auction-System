About Us

E-Sports Pvt Ltd is well known for conducting Cricket Player auctions in Sri Lanka. A team can retain a player to their team by bidding more points than the other team. if anyone does not start the bid on a player then the player is unsold.
Software Features :

  • Live Player Profile with attractive design.
  • Live Team Bid.
  • Effective Sold Out Screen.
  • Auto Record Unsold player list.

The easy to understand functionalities are also present in the E-Sports Auction Website. With help of this player auction web application, anybody can know about the teams, team owners, management, team rules and player auction process.
